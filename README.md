# MinMaxTicTacToe

A sophisticated 4x4 Tic-Tac-Toe game featuring AI opponents with multiple difficulty levels, piece removal mechanics, and strategic gameplay.

## 🎮 Features

- **4x4 Game Board** - Larger than traditional Tic-Tac-Toe for more strategic depth
- **Three AI Difficulty Levels** - From random moves to advanced strategic AI
- **Unique Piece Removal Mechanic** - Each player can only have 5 pieces on the board at once
- **Background Music Support** - Play YouTube audio while gaming
- **Console-Based Interface** - Clean, text-based display with position numbering

## 🎯 How to Play

### Game Rules
- Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) on a 4x4 grid
- The first player to get 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
- Each player can only have **5 pieces on the board at once**
- When placing a 6th piece, your oldest piece is automatically removed
- Strategic removal of pieces adds a unique tactical element!

### Controls
- Choose positions by entering numbers 0-15 (displayed on the board)
- The board shows position numbers for easy reference
